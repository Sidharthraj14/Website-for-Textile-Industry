About us

VISWAS TEXTILE HARNESSING

Services on setting up of the traditional hand loom and setup from the scratch . Viswas textile harnessing is an technical start-up under the scheme of SHRI,DST, to support other weavers in setting up of loom and to be a helping hand in the harnessing process of loom setup for each type of saree & fabric weaving structure. Main focus of the start-up is to be the educational source for the upcoming younger generation of weavers and to bring out the awareness on the technical knowledge about the weaving process to the society.

Mr.Govindas, with his 35 years of experience in weaving and specialized in harnessing of traditional hand loom, jacquard loom and e-loom is now working to develop harnessing curriculum to provide the technical need from the peer weaving community. He is also well versed in setting up of Kutni based loom and has developed a harnessing curriculam for setting up of Kutni loom
